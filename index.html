ABOUT US

VIVENSA MG IMPEX PRIVATE LIMITED specialises in consumer electronics distribution and trade. Our expertise comes from our Singapore base, in providing innovative consumer electronic products and solutions that cater to the consumer needs with a strong customer base across Southeast Asian region.

We are the sole distributor for AGFAPHOTO products for India and appointed as manufacturer representative to distribute all product range.

Over the years we have gained the respect and trust of our partners by developing healthy, trusted and long-term relationships.

We are committed to be the leading distributor of AGFAPHOTO products specialising in sales and marketing and providing complete customer-focused solutions with our dedication, perseverance and insatiable endeavors for total customer satisfaction.

Our Brand

AGFAPHOTO, a pioneer in photography

Founded in 1867, AGFA (now Agfa-Gevaert) has become a leading producer and consistent innovator of consumer imaging worldwide. For several decades, AGFA held more than 10% of the global market share in the most important consumer imaging and photography product categories.

Dating back over 150 years, AgfaPhoto is an iconic brand unlike any other in the photography market. Agfa-Gevaert sold their Consumer Imaging division to AgfaPhoto Gmbh. The holding firm AgfaPhoto Holding Gmbh headquartered in Leverkusen and Cologne/Germany, licensed GT Company, headquartered in Chessy, France to design, manufacture, and distribute the AgfaPhoto consumer imaging products. We hold the rights from our French Partner GT Company, to distribute and sell the products in India.
